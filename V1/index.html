Bridging a Cultural Gap

While English books for toddlers abound, quality Hindi books that engage young minds are surprisingly scarce, especially for families living abroad.

Chakri creates beautiful, modern bilingual books that make learning Hindi a joyful experience for the youngest readers, helping parents pass on their language and heritage effortlessly.

A Mother's Inspiration

Chakri was born when a software engineer and mother noticed her toddler son had plenty of English books but none in Hindiâ€”their mother tongue.

After creating a single Hindi book for her son, she witnessed his joy as he treasured it, carrying it everywhere and asking for repeated readings. When family and friends eagerly requested copies, she realized countless other parents faced the same challenge.

Today, Chakri exists to help all Indian families nurture a love for their native language from the very beginning.

Frequently Asked Questions

What age group are your books designed for?

Our board books are specially designed for children aged 0-4 years, with durable pages and engaging content appropriate for early learners.

When will the books be available for purchase?

We're planning to launch our first collection in Summer 2025. Join our waitlist to be the first to know when pre-orders open.

Do I need to know Hindi to use these books with my child?

Not at all! Our books are designed with both Hindi and English text, plus simple pronunciation guides, making them accessible even for parents who aren't fluent in Hindi.

Will you ship internationally?

Yes! We understand many Indian families live around the world, and we'll be shipping to major international destinations.

Are digital versions of the books available?

We're focusing on physical board books initially, but we're exploring digital companions for the future. Sign up to our waitlist for updates.

Will you create books in other Indian languages?

Our initial focus is on Hindi, but we plan to expand to other Indian languages based on community interest. Let us know which languages you'd like to see!
